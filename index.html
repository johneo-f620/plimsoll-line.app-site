Visualize Your Capacity

The "water line" give you where you are with your emotional limit. The higher the water line, the more you need to give attention to your emotional well-being. Red tasks give you anxiety, the blue ones offset the anxiety with joy. The water line rises or falls based based on the sum of anxieties and joys, giving you the big picture of when and how you can find emotional balance.

Works With Your Life

Plimsoll Line seamlessly syncs with Apple Reminders. Add tasks in Reminders, and they'll appear in the app, ready for you to prioritize. No need to change your workflow.
